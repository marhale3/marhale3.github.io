 Let d(n) be the number of divisors of n.  
 Let M(n,k) be the maximum value of d(j) for n ≤ j ≤ n+k-1.  
 Let S(u,k) be the sum of M(n,k) for 1 ≤ n ≤ u-k+1.

 You are given that S(1000,10)=17176.

 Find S(100 000 000,100 000).
